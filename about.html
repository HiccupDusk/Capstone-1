ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Shuaib Abdurahman, and I'm presently enrolled in Zuitt Bootcamp to pursue a career as a software developer. I was once a cadet officer in the military, having completed my training at the prestigious Philippine Military Academy in Bagiou City, Philippines. But, because I wasn't interested in the military and instead wanted to pursue a career as a software developer, I opted to change my career path from cadet officer in training to software developer.

One of the reasons I changed careers is that software engineering is such a broad subject that encompasses a wide range of professions using computer applications and systems. This gives you the opportunity to work in the field or fields that you are most passionate about. And I've always been fascinated by technological advancements.

Software developers learn more quickly than other professions. This makes sense since, while change occurs quickly in many domains, it occurs more rapidly in the field of technology.
