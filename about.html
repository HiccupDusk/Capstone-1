ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Shuaib Abdurahman, and I'm presently enrolled in Zuitt Bootcamp to pursue a career as a software developer. I was once a cadet officer in the military, having completed my training at the prestigious Philippine Military Academy in Bagiou City, Philippines. But, because I wasn't interested in the military and instead wanted to pursue a career as a software developer, I opted to change my career path from cadet officer in training to software developer.

One of the reasons I changed my professional path is because software engineering is such a vast subject that spans a wide range of professions linked to both computer applications and systems. This allows you to work in the field or fields that most interest you. And technology has always been a source of fascination for me.

Software developers learn at a faster rate than other occupations. This makes sense since, while change happens quickly in many sectors, it happens more quickly in the realm of technology.
